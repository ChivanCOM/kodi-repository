# iBroadcast Music for Kodi

iBroadcast Music is an iBroadcast player for Kodi 21+, allowing you to stream your entire music library directly from the cloud — just like on your other devices.

With integrated artist information, fanart, and album metadata, you can explore your music collection in a richer and more engaging way. Discover more about your favorite artists and albums while enjoying seamless playback within Kodi.

## Features

- Access and play your complete iBroadcast music library within Kodi, including playlists
- Adjustable playback bitrate up to original quality (original quality requires an active iBroadcast subscription)
- Built-in support for artist biographies and album descriptions, powered by MusicBrainz, TheAudioDB, Discogs, and the iBroadcast API
- Smooth playback experience, ideal for gapless transitions
- "Up Next" and queue functionality to easily manage upcoming tracks
- Library refresh options to fetch new metadata for your full library or newly added artists/albums
- Advanced search with results categorized by song, album, or artist

- In some cases, crashes may occur when changing songs
